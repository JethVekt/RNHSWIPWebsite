About The Rosales National High School:

Rosales National High School (RNHS) is a public secondary educational institution located in Rosales, Pangasinan.The school is currently implementing a K to 12 basic education program offering both junior and senior high school. It is in accordance with the resolution that was ordered by the Department of Education (DepEd).




The RNHS Vision:

The Rosales National High School seeks to build a better future for generations to come by teaching and training today's generation a career path



The RNHS Mission:

The Rosales National High School aim to provide quality and affordable education to the residents of Rosales, Pangasinan, as well as educate and train future professionals. The Senior High School section offers multiple strands for the SHS Students in order to better understand what career path should they take before attending College as they graduate.
